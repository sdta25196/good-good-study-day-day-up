# 情态动词否定

  情态动词 + not + 动词原形

  He can't sing an english sing

  He may not know her

  He mustn't go there

  He doesn't have to go there // 助动词

# 情态动词提问

情态动词 + 主语 + 动词原形  

Can he sing an english song?
- yes, he can

Must he go here?
- no, he needn't

Does he have to go here?
- yes, he does
